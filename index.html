Brief company description

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is one of India’s largest IT services firms and part of the Tata Group. It helps businesses worldwide with software development, technology consulting, cloud and digital transformation, and business process services. Clients span banking, retail, healthcare, and manufacturing; TCS operates across many countries and is known for large-scale delivery of technology projects and long-term outsourcing partnerships. The company is listed on Indian stock exchanges and is widely followed in the IT sector.
